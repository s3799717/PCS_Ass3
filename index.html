PROJECT DESCRIPTION

What does it do?

Noting to wear application will use technologies such as Augmented reality to try clothes in virtual world and QR code scanning technologies to identify clothes and upload to the system. GPS technology will be used to identify the user location and get the weather information to continue the process. These technologies will help this application to identify the clothes available and match outfits for the user while checking the compatibility with the location and event. This application will be a new era to the fashion industry as it gives more than other competitive applications which are in the market such dressing the avatar to match outfits.

This application will help the fashionable young ladies to match outfits from their virtual world to real world through AR and VR technology. Augmented reality and Virtual reality will help users to connect with virtual world to process the requests through the application. GPS and QR scanning technologies will be used to process the requests of the user and will be allowed to input relevant data through the process to give the result with combining AR VR technology. Matching outfits with information of the events and weather will be a huge process and giving the result through dressing an avatar is a complicated and will deliver a quality result at the end to the user.

In next 3 years the technologies will be more developed such as AR VR technology is skyrocketing through recent years to give users massive results in the fashion industry. AR VR technology can be used with AR devices to check the outfits with more details in the future to give users next level of experience while matching the outfits. Creating the avatar with the real face of the user will be a big challenge to develop in the process but a great achievement to have soon. GPS technology will be expanded with 5G improvements and will be easy to identify the location instantly with the speed of the powerful technologies. Shopping section will be created to help users to shop the suggested clothing items and will help users to match outfits for their needs.

AR VR Technology will be used to enhance the avatar experience with the user and this technology will be mostly used to project the view on the screen. AR VR technology is becoming more intelligent in next years and it can be used to enhance the experience of the users by enhancing the application itself. Virtual Reality will satisfy user needs without even trying the outfits in reality with more details and in voice command in future with developing the application to that level. Giving the chance to transform the avatar to the face of the user will make the virtual environment more real to the users and it will beneficial to the application by attracting more users to the application in the long run. 5G technology will be improved more and will allow the application to act fast while identifying the location and syncing the calendars. E commerce platform will be used to create the shopping section and it will be enhanced with product visualization to shop easily for the users of the application.

What is the likely impact?

These developments in the application will be impacted to the users and they will be satisfied using the application with a new experience of the virtual world. The users will be young fashionable ladies who love to do fashion and ladies who has a tight schedule. It will be easy to manage their outfit with this application and with the developments it will be easier. Users will have the real feel of using the new avatar with the development and they will feel that they are trying the outfits on themselves. Shopping section will make easier to shop for the users and improved 5G technology will make easier the whole process. Time of the users will be saved as they can get everything, they need to lead their fashionable life with a click away.

More developers will be needed to improve the application features and more staff will be needed to manage the e platform as well. Clothing companies should be contacted to work with the e platform and there will be more jobs in the industry for the e platform as e commerce involves in the process. More jobs will be created for in the clothing industry such deliveries, packaging, fashion designers, fashion merchandisers etc.

How will this affect you?

As a young girl who likes to do fashion, this application developments will amaze me to try it. The avatar feeling will be real even when I am busy working or studying. It will be easy to manage my closet with this developed application and I will get a big picture of the clothes I have even when I am on my university lunch break. Shopping platform will be great with suggesting clothes that I should buy, will make me aware which items I need, and it will opt me out from buying similar items.

My mother will be amazed with her busy lifestyle to use this developed application without checking her wardrobe every time when she goes out. It will make it easy to shop clothes with the shopping platform even when she is waiting to finish the baking cake in the oven. Her time and costs will be saved, and her tasks will be eased. My friends who love fashion will get more used to this developed application as it will give a great experience with the avatar. Making their own avatar with their real face will be a great result which can make them happy by matching their own outfits through this application. Matching outfits with more details in the virtual world will make them thrilled to use this application more often.
